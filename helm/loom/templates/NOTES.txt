Loom is installed as {{ include "loom.fullname" . }}.

Port-forward the GraphQL server:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "loom.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }}

Then open:
  http://127.0.0.1:8080/healthz
  http://127.0.0.1:8080/graphql

This release includes an ArangoDB service managed by the Loom chart.
All data is lost when the release is removed.
